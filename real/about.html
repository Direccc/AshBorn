WHAT IS ASHBORN

01
THE ASB CULTURE

With offices in Philippines as well as remote employees across the globe, we're a passionate group of people from all over, who are united by our love of gaming, esports, and of course, winning. Our collaborative, dynamic culture provides employees the opportunity to work cross-functionally and efficiently. AshBorn is a global esports joint venture that owns and operates the ultra-successful AB Valorant Champions APAC (Asia-Pacific) team, along with teams in competitive gaming segments including Dota 2, League of Legends, Wild Rift, Overwatch, and Halo. Building upon the rich legacy of AshBorn, AB will expand its teams globally, celebrate new victories, and create even more opportunities for fans to embrace AB’s gaming culture, content, and players in every corner of the world.






02
STRIVE FOR EXCELLENCE

We're working to build one of the most successful and recognizable esports organizations in the world, and are satisfied with nothing less than being the best. As a company we take great pride in our victories and put the competitive integrity, sportsmanship, and performance of our teams and players above all else. Through the leadership of the 3 founders, namely Jiyan, Direcc and Astrid, the dreams of many are just the start for the AB's employees.



03
RAISING THE BAR

We try to approach everything we do thoughtfully and creatively. Life as a TSM employee is collaborative, energetic, productive, and genuinely fun. Our employees enjoy free delicious food, top-tier amenities, great community, and a vibrant atmosphere where everyone strives for greatness. If this all sounds great and fits how you want to work, we’d love to hear from you!

ASB is the worlds leading esports team. We strive in triumph for everything we do.
